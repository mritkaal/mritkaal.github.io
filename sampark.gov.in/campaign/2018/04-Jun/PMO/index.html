INTERNATIONAL ENGAGEMENTS

PM Modi makes successful Singapore visit, delivers keynote address at Shangri La Dialogue

On a visit to Singapore, PM Modi held productive talks with Singapore PM Lee Hsien Loong to strengthen bilateral relations. Delivering the keynote address at the prestigious Shangri La Dialogue, PM Modi said inclusiveness, openness and ASEAN centrality and unity lie at the heart of the new Indo-Pacific. He also added that India does not see the Indo-Pacific Region as a strategy or as a club of limited members.
