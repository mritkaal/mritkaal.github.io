Addressing the Conclave of Accountants General and Deputy Accountants General and said that CAG should act as a catalyst of good governance. Emphasizing that time-bound and outcome based system of working was developing in the country PM Modi said CAG had a great role in that.

PM addresses special discussion to mark 250th Session of Rajya Sabha

Addressing a special discussion to mark 25th Session of the Rajya Sabha, PM Modi said not only the Upper House made significant contribution to the history of the country but also has seen history being made. He said the vision of the makers of Indian Constitution in creating a bicameral legislative framework enriched our democracy.

Let us channelize best solutions which come out of debates for betterment of the country: PM

At the start of the Winter Session of the Parliament, PM Modi appealed to all the MPs for active and positive participation in the various debates. The Prime Minister said it is necessary we have great debates either for or against and channelize the best solutions which come out of it for the betterment and welfare of the country.

Bill Gates applauds PM Modi for prioritizing nutrition as a key focus area

Mr. Bill Gates met Prime Minister Modi in Delhi. Mr. Gates commended the Prime Minister on prioritizing nutrition as a key focus area and the efforts being made under the National Nutrition Mission. He extended his Foundation’s support in Government of India’s efforts to meet the Sustainable Development Goals, with a particular focus on health and sanitation.

PM’s “excellent interaction” with Tony Abbott

Former PM of Australia, Mr. Tony Abbott met Prime Minister Narendra Modi in Delhi. Taking to Twitter, PM Modi said that he had an “excellent interaction” with Tony Abbott. PM Modi lauded Mr. Abbott’s passion and energy and acknowledged his role in strengthening India-Australia relations.
